Toms River Bookjacket

This book is a scientific and journalist investigation on industrial pollution and childhood cancer cases. I did many sketches, but decided to proceed with the one of realistic features. Any drawing could give the impression that the investigation is not authentic. My design elements are therefore based on real photos. The result is an artistic, simple yet strong, and convincing depiction of the book.
