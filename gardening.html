Gardening Society Logo

This logo is not about products but about knowledge. It is a stylized logo design with realistic elements and a professional impression for a knowledge-sharing organization. To create this logo, I studied the logos of botanic gardens and native gardening societies. Their logos are all based on real plants. These logos inspired me, but I wanted to incorporate design principles into my logo. Hence are these sketches, rough drafts, and the final design.
