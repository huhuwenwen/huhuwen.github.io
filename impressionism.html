Impressionism Booklet

The booklet on Impressionism is a work of intensive research, engaging writing, meticulous image selection, and a balanced layout. I wanted to present the famous art movement differently. I composed this article focusing on understanding the paintings and their historical significance. I collected high-resolution images in the public domain suitable for printing. I used round frames to create focal points, and carefully laid out the text and the images according to industry standards. This is the process of my work.
