Indian Horse Movie Poster

Indian Horse is a Canadian movie about the life of a talented Indigenous ice hockey player who survived the cruel residential school system. How to deliver the essence of the movie without deterring a potential audience? I combined indigenous art, symbolism, and storytelling into one appealing design. This mindmap of important scenes and symbols helped me create a series of sketches, which led to the rough draft and this final design.
